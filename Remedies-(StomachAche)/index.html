Stomach Ache

Stomach aches can stem from various factors like indigestion, gas, constipation, infections, ulcers, food intolerances, stress, or underlying conditions such as appendicitis or gastritis.

Ease stomach ache with ginger tea, peppermint, warm compresses, chamomile tea, or apple cider vinegar. Stay hydrated, rest, and avoid spicy or greasy foods.
